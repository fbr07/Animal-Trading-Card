Lion

Adult lions have 30 teeth. They use their teeth in order to hunt and protect themselves.

Sleeping Schedule: Lions are the laziest of all the big cats, and can sleep for 16-20 hours per day!
Life Span: Male lions have an average lifespan of 8-10 years. Female lions have an average lifespan of 15-16 years.
Habitat: The African lion is found in Africa, south of the Sahara Desert. The Asiatic lion exists in one small population around western India.
Species: There are is only species of lion, which is known scientifically as Panthera leo.

A lion also known as the the King of the Jungle, is a fearless mammal. A lion is a large well-muscled cat with a long body, large head and short head.
